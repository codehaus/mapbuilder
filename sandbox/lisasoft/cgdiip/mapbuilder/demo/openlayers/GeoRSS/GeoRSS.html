Explanation

This demonstrates a GeoRSS reader and renderer.

In particular it demonstrates how a OpenLayers GeoRSS parser and renderer can be integrated into mapbuilder.

The vectors are rendered using OpenLayers and the listing is displayed using a XSL transfer which directly transfers the GeoRSS into the list.
