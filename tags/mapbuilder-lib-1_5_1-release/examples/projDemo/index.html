MapBuilder Proj4js demo

Explanation

This demo shows the Proj4js library in action. All coordinate conversions shown here are being carried out on the client side in JavaScript.

The cursor is tracked in Longitude and Latitude as well as in map projected X-Y coordinates.

A GML document may be loaded and its feature coordinates will be converted to the map projection prior to being rendered over the map.
